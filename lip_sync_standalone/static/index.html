🤖 Creating Rhubarb Sprite Sheets with AI (Nana Bana Pro)

You can generate custom high-quality mouth sprite sheets matching your character using image generators like Nana Bana Pro. Upload an image of your character as style base, and prompt for sprite sheets at different angles:

Front View (0°):
"This is my charcater i am doing manual animations now i need sprites to use for rhubarb lip sync tools, so give rhubrb sprites, skin colour must be accurate."
3/4 Angle (45° / -45°):
"now mouth sprites for side pose at 45 degree"
Profile Angle (90° / -90°):
"now mouth sprites for side pose at 90 degree"
⚠️ Background Removal & Grid Alignment Warning Generated sprite sheets will contain a background and labels. You must remove the background to make it transparent:
  • Use PhotoRoom Background Remover (highly recommended: preserves original sheet resolution).
  • Or use remove.bg.
CRITICAL: Background removal removes text labels. The mouth cells in the uploaded sheet must follow the exact 5x2 grid format matching the template:
  • Row 1: Visemes A, B, C, D, E (from left to right)
  • Row 2: Visemes F, G, H, X, and Neutral (from left to right)
