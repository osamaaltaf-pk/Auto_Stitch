The Workflow

Four-step
production pipeline.

From raw clips to a polished final export — AutoStitch handles every step locally. Your footage never leaves your machine. AI models run on your GPU, on your schedule.

01
🎬
Scan Media

Load a local folder. All .mp4 clips auto-populate Lane 1, sorted and ready for composition.

02
🎵
Convert SFX

Write prompt descriptions for each clip to synthesize custom music or sound effects (SFX) locally to make your content alive and premium.

03
🎤
Narrate Voice

Type scripts or clone voices in 6 premium languages (English, French, German, Spanish, Portuguese, Italian) to generate natural speech for every scene in seconds.

04
🚀
Master Render

Preview in sync, then FFmpeg stitches video + SFX + voice into per-clip or concatenated master files.
